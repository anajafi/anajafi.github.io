Ali Najafi

I am a Ph.D. candidate working with Shyam Gollakota in the Networks and Mobile Systems Lab. Currently, I am working on various topics including low power wireless networks and sensing systems, embedded systems and mobile health. I am also experienced in analog/mixed-signal/RF integrated circuit design. During my Ph.D., I interned at Qualcomm (2016), Apple (2017) and Jeeva Wireless (2018).
Before coming to UW, I did my bachelors in Sharif University of Technology, Iran working on gyro-magnetic photonic crystals with Sina Khorasani. I also did my masters in the same department where I worked with Mehrdad Sharif-Bakhtiar on low power RFID tags for wireless remote sensing applications.
